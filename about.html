Dora Yi-Chen Weng

I am currently a first-year Master's student in the School of Information at the University of Michigan, focusing on human-computer interaction, social computing, and UX design.

Prior to studying at UM, I was a UX consultant at SAP, which launched my wonderful UX journey. I am passionate about understanding people's workflows on a variety of contexts and streamlining the flows and interactions with simplified designs and visualized data. I believe a crafted rendering with user-centered designs can help people make better decisions.

With a Master's degree in Materials Engineering, I had been a semiconductor engineer for 3 years. I switched my career because I aspire to have a direct impact on people's thoughts and needs.

Coming from a diverse background, I am always open-minded and embrace any possibility!
